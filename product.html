FLAT ROOF WATERPROOFING


Permaphalt Polymer Modified Mastic Asphalt for use as a waterproofing layer on flat roofs manufactured under a Quality Assurance Scheme to
BS EN ISO 9002:1994. Permaphalt has been independently assessed by the Building Research Establishment, Department of Civil Engineering at Bristol University and is covered by the British Board of Agrement - Certificate Number 89 / 2299.

Permaphalt is suitable for the majority of flat roofing situations, particularly insulated decks and is installed by approved contractors and covered by a 15 year single point guarantee supplied by Permanite Asphalt. Every Permaphalt specification is backed by a comprehensive technical advisory service. This includes initial site survey, detailed written specification, site visits and client liaison.



FLOOD DEFENCE


Bituminous Grout

Permanite Asphalt is able to manufacture, deliver and install a wide range of bituminous grouts for use on sea walls, river and canal embankments, channel linings and underground works.

Permanite Asphalt operates its own self - offload vehicles for blocks and also a fleet of bulk transporters which heat and agitate the molten grout for delivery direct to site. This form of delivery provides large daily quantities of material which considerably shortens contract durations.

Permanite Asphalt has considerable contracting expertise coupled with specialist plant and equipment.

Open Stone Asphalt

A gap-graded mixture of mastic and stone, a formula for flood defence of rivers and estuaries. Its nature and viscosity lends itself to smoothly following the contours of the revetment.

Sand Asphalt

A mixture of sand and bitumen which can be used as core material, filter layer or cover layer above or below water level. It is also an economic and efficient method of providing EMERGENCY repairs when existing flood defences are breached.



ASPHALT & CONCRETE CRACK REPAIR SYSTEM


Permatrack - System 1

System 1 produces a permanent seal and bond for the more serious cracks in both asphalt and concrete surfaces.

Designated cracks are typically cut out to a width of 200mm and a depth of 30mm. The channel being cleaned, dried and primed using Permatrack base material which penetrates, seals and bonds any underlying fractures.

High PSV stone is then incorporated into the Permatrack base material and laid into the channel flush with the surface, again bonding and sealing the affected areas.

Permatrack - System 2

System 2 is a simple, quick and effective system for over bonding and sealing cracks and joints in the road surface, whether it be concrete or asphalt, using a flexible high softening point material which penetrates into the crack as well as the channel. The area is prepared, cleaned, dried and filled flush to the road surface with Permatrack System 2 material.
